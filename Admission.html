The admission process for the Master of Computer Applications (MCA) and Master of Business Administration (MBA) programs is conducted in accordance with the norms set by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and Osmania University. Candidates are admitted through two main categories:

üü¢ 1. Convener Quota (70%)

  • 70% of the total intake is filled based on the merit secured in the Integrated Common Entrance Test (ICET).
  • ICET is conducted by the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE).
  • Seat allotment is handled by the ICET Convener through centralized counseling.
  • Candidates must meet eligibility criteria set by the university and state authorities.

üü† 2. Management Quota (30%)

  • The remaining 30% of seats are filled under the Management/NRI quota.
  • Admissions are carried out by the institution as per APSCHE and Osmania University guidelines.
  • Selection is based on merit, academic background, and any screening/interview process conducted by the institute.

üìå Key Points to Note

  • A bachelor's degree (minimum 3 years duration) is required.
  • Minimum aggregate percentage must meet university criteria.
  • The institute follows a transparent, fair, and merit-based admission process.
  • All reservations and quotas are applied as per government regulations.

üìù Application Process

  • Students applying through the Convener Quota should register for ICET via the official APSCHE website.
  • Candidates applying under the Management Quota should contact the college office directly for details, application forms, and deadlines.
