Shadan Institute of Computer Studies.

Established in 1991, Shadan Institute of Computer Studies is a prestigious institution committed to providing quality higher education in the fields of Computer Applications and Business Administration. The college is open to both girls and boys, fostering a diverse and inclusive learning environment.

We Offer:

1. A Two-year Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program

2. A Two-year Master of Business Administration (MBA) program Each with an intake of 60 students, both approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), affiliated to Osmania University, and recognized by the Government of Telangana.
